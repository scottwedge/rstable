$w or $wallet|checks your own wallet and displays rs3, 07, and ticket balance.
$w (@USER) or $wallet (@USER)|checks the given user's wallet and displays their rs3, 07, and ticket balance.
$transfer (@USER) (AMOUNT) (rs3 or 07)|transfers the given user that amount of money from your wallet to theirs.
$privacy (on or off)|toggles wallet privacy mode. (Stops other users from looking at your wallet)
$clear (rs3 or 07) (@USER) - ADMIN|clears the given user's wallet of rs3 or 07 currency.
$deposit (@USER) (AMOUNT) (rs3 or 07) - ADMIN|adds that amount of money to the given user's wallet.
$withdraw (@USER) (AMOUNT) (rs3 or 07) - ADMIN|removes that amount of money from the given user's wallet.
$ticket (@USER) (AMOUNT) - ADMIN|adds that amount of tickets to the given user's wallet.
$wager, $total bet, or $tb|displays your total wager (The sum of all bets you've ever placed)
$thisweek|displays your wager for this week - resets each Monday.
$leaderboard (rs3 or 07) (total or weekly)|displays the top 8 users with the highest wagers.
$reset thisweek - ADMIN|manually resets weekly wager.
$k or $keys|displays how many keys you have of each type.
$buykey (AMOUNT) (KEY TYPE)|buys that amonut of the given key type. (250k Bronze, 750k Silver, 2m Gold)
$open (KEY TYPE)|opens a chest of the given key type, consuming one key.
$weekly - SUBSCRIPTION REQUIRED|adds 5 keys to your balance; the type depends on what subscription tier you have.
$giftkey (@USER) (KEY TYPE)|opens a chest of the given key type and gifts the prize to the specified user, consuming one key.
$updatekey (@USER) (KEY TYPE) (AMOUNT) - ADMIN|updates the number of keys the given user has.
$50 (BET) (rs3 or 07)|starts a provably fair dicing game - roll over 50 to win, x1.8 payout.
$53 (BET) (rs3 or 07)|starts a provably fair dicing game - roll over 53 to win, x2 payout.
$75 (BET) (rs3 or 07)|starts a provably fair dicing game - roll over 75 to win, x3 payout.
$95 (BET) (rs3 or 07)|starts a provably fair dicing game - roll over 95 to win, x7 payout.
$bj (BET) (rs3 or 07)|starts a blackjack game against the bot ai.
$fp (AMOUNT) (rs3 or 07)|starts a flower poker game against the bot.
$please|starts a roulette game.
bet (CATEGORY) (AMOUNT)|adds a bet to the roulette wheel.
$add (AMOUNT)|adds the given amount to the jackpot.
$jackpot (AMOUNT) - ADMIN|changes the amount at which the jackpot game ends.
$endjackpot - ADMIN|manually ends a jackpot game.
$commands|displays the command list. (Obviously)
$help (COMMAND NAME)|gives a description of the given command. (Woah this is meta)
$rank|displays your current rank and xp in a beautiful visual.
$levels|displays the top 10 users with the highest xp.
$setseed (SEED)|changes your client seed for provably fair gambling.
$userinfo (@USER)|displays stalker-like information about the given user.
$poll (QUESTION)|creates a poll with the given question.
$colorpicker|displays a random color and hexadecimal value.
$drawraffle - ADIMN|draws a user for the monthly 100m raffle and resets everyone's ticket count.
